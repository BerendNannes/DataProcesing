Religious Diversity Index

Distribution Unfold

The cloropleth map represents the country's religious diversity index (RDI). The closer a country comes to having equal shares of different religions, the higher its score on a 10-point Religious Diversity Index. Clicking on a country will reveal the distribution of the different religions and some of it's denominations. Hovering over the partitions will show more detailed population numbers.

Data Sources: PewResearch, ARDA.
